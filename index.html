WordFinder

WordFinder: a simple game that puts your vocabulary and pattern recognition abilities to the test. Race against the clock as you try to find the hidden words in as many random sets of letters as you can. Beat your best score! And set the bar for your friends.

How to Play

How to play

First choose your game settings: play with 7 or 8 letters, and against the clock for either 3m, 5m, 10m, or with no timer at all.

Click start, to generate a random set of letter tiles. Try to make up to 10, 4+ letter words. To make a word, select a letter tile by clicking it, and then click one of the letter tile holders, to place that tile in that position. Click the reset tiles button to reset placed letters. To submit a word, click the check word button. Struggling to find up to 10 words? Click the next word button to generate a new set of letter tiles, as often as you like.

You receive 1pt per letter in every correct word you make. The best score displayed is for the current selected game settings. Enjoy!

Note if you are playing with the sound on mode, please ensure your browser is set to allow autoplaying of sounds.
